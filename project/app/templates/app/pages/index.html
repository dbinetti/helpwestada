Help West Ada!

On Tuesday February 9th, the West Ada School Board voted 4-1 to bring the kids all in school, Full-Time, In-Person, K-12 by March 29 (moved up from the original date of April 13.) This timetable — while supported by the District — produces operational gaps that parent volunteers can fill.

This site was created to match open roles to willing participants. By simplyfing the process and adding a bit of social benefit, we hope that it will become a lot easier to Help West Ada.

We are currently working with the WASD to determine the best way to include volunteers. Please register to be notified when positions become available. Registration is free, your personal information is protected, and you can help us keep the kids all in school.
