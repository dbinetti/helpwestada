Help West Ada!

On Tuesday February 9th, the West Ada School Board voted 4-1 to bring the kids all in school, Full-Time, In-Person, K-12 by March 29 (moved up from the original date of April 13.) This timetable — while supported by the District — does produce some operational gaps that we as parent volunteers can help fill.

This site was created as a clearinghouse to help match open roles to willing participants. By simplyfing the process and adding a bit of social benefit, we hope that it will become a lot easier to Help West Ada.

Please join us! The site is free, your personal information is protected, and you can help us keep the kids all in school.
