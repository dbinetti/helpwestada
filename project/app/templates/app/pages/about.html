About

Help West Ada is a group of parent substitute teachers who wish to volunteer on an as-needed basis. We support a simplified, streamlined process for parents to help when no other professional resources are available.

Help West Ada is a self-funded, volunteer intitiatve produced in partnership with the West Ada School District.
