About

My name is David Binetti, I'm a parent of two West Ada students, and I created this site to keep our kids all in school. The move from hybrid to full-time instruction will create operational gaps that parents and volunteers can fill and in so doing help West Ada School District keep the classrooms open.

I have no political agenda, the site is totally free, and protects your privacy. I just want to Help West Ada, and if you agree please join me.
